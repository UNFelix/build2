Step 2 - Bear vs Bull

Bulls are traders and investors who make money on the price rise. Bears are market participants who are confident in its fall. When there are more buyers than sellers, the market rises and is called bullish. When there are more sellers in the market than buyers, the price starts to fall. A falling market is called bearish.

Step 4 - Quotes and prices

Usually, quotes are displayed on the chart as so called candles. Each candlestick has a body and a shadow corresponding to prices at the beginning and end of the time period and the minimum and maximum price values ​​for the specified time period. All price charts move from left to right. A new candlestick corresponds to each new period of time.
